🤔 What's a Trust Battery?

⚡
💚

The trust battery is a Shopify concept that Tobi Lütke introduced to describe how working relationships function. Every relationship between two people has a metaphorical battery that can be charged or drained based on their interactions.

When you start working with someone new, the battery begins at about 50% — neutral. Every time you deliver on a commitment, respond promptly, give credit, or close loops, the battery charges. When you miss deadlines, leave messages unanswered, or break promises, it drains.

"Trust is like a battery that exists between buyers, suppliers, merchants, partners, apps, and ourselves."
— Tobi Lütke, 2022 Themes

✨ Why We Built ShopiTrust

We built ShopiTrust because trust is often invisible. You might have a sense that a relationship is strained, but it's hard to pinpoint why. Or you might not realize you've been draining someone's trust battery until it's already low.

ShopiTrust makes the invisible visible by analyzing real signals from your interactions:

💬 Response times on Slack
✅ Commitments kept vs. broken
📅 Meeting patterns & cancellations
🎉 Recognition & shoutouts given
🔄 Loops closed vs. left hanging
📧 Email follow-through

Then we give you one specific, actionable tip to improve — not generic advice, but something grounded in what actually happened.

💪 Benefits of Being Trustworthy

🏃‍♂️
Move Faster
High-trust teams skip the overhead of verification and second-guessing. Decisions happen faster because people trust each other's judgment.
🎯
Get Better Feedback
When people trust you, they give you honest feedback instead of holding back. That's how you grow.
🤝
Attract Opportunities
People want to work with trustworthy colleagues. High trust batteries lead to more interesting projects and collaborations.
😌
Less Stress
When you've built trust, you don't need to over-explain or worry about how things will be perceived. You can just focus on the work.
🌱
Compounding Returns
Trust builds on itself. One kept promise makes the next one easier to believe. It's the best investment you can make in your career.
🛡️
Resilience in Hard Times
When things go wrong (and they will), high trust gives you the benefit of the doubt. People assume good intent.
